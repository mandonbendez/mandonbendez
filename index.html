BRANDON MENDEZ

Guitar Performance and Instruction

Brandon Mendez is a Los Angeles-based guitarist whose abilities encompass a variety of genres and styles, making him one of the most eclectic musicians performing today. From classical to rock and all the way to jazz he is always sure to leave the audience craving more of his delicate yet compelling playing style.

In addition to his international performance experience, Brandon has been providing music for private events, such as weddings and private dinners, for years. Furthermore, Brandon also has been giving private and group lessons for over 5 years and currently teaches in studios around the San Fernando Valley.

Under scholarship, Brandon received his Bachelor of Music degree in Guitar Performance in May of 2016, studying with Steven Thachuk and Grammy award winning guitarist John Dearman. Brandon is currently pursuing his Masters of Music degree in Guitar Performance at California State University Northridge.
